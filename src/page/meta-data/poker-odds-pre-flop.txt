Poker odds pre-flop heads-up
Shows the precise odds for any pre-flop heads-up situation in Texas Hold'em poker.
poker;texas hold'em;heads-up;pre-flop;odds;probabilities
A Texas Hold'em poker deck contains 52 different cards, in a heads-up game two players are playing against each other. Both of them get two cards dealt pre-flop face down. There are 1326 different possible pairs of cards that players can get -- the best one being two Aces.
